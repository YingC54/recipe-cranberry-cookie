Chewy Oatmeal Cranberry Cookies Recipe

Chef: Ying Chen

Ingredients:

1 egg
1 1/2 cups regular oats (375 mL)
3/4 cup organic all-purpose flour (or non-organic flour) (175 mL)
3/4 cup brown sugar (175 mL)
1/2 cup unsalted margarine, softened (or butter) (125 mL)
1/2 tsp baking soda (2 mL)
1/2 tsp baking powder (2 mL)
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (2 mL)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract (2 mL)
1/2 cup Hershey Pure Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips (125ml)
1 tbsp chia seeds (15 mL)
1/4 cup dried cranberries (50 mL)
1/2 cup pumpkin seeds (50 mL)
some salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line baking sheet with parchment paper or silicone mat.
  2. Beat softened, unsalted margarine or butter with brown sugar and vanilla extract until creamy. Add 1 egg and beat until combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix oats with organic all-purpose flour or non-organic flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Gradually add in the oat flour mixture into the butter mixture just until combined.
  4. Gently fold in Hershey's Pure Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips, chia seeds, cranberries and pumpkin seeds.
  5. Scoop 1 tbsp (15 mL) of cookie batter onto prepared baking sheet, placing 2” (5 cm) apart.
  6. Bake for 12-14 minutes. Transfer to wire rack to cool completely.

Equipment Best Practices and Tips:

  • Oven (remember to preheat before baking)
  • Parchment paper
  • Mixer
  • Wire rack
  • Cookie sheet (count for use of serving of 24 cookies as per this recipe)
  • Measuring cups (include measuring for teaspoons)
  • Mixing bowl
